Smart Ops Control Center

"The screen was manageable; the environment was the bottleneck."

The Discovery

Management identifying "UI overload." After 3 full days of ethnographic shadowing across 24h cycles, I discovered that slow reaction times were actually caused by constant foot-traffic distractions and light glare.

User Research & VR

Using VR prototyping, I simulated the entire control center redesign before a single desk was moved. This allowed pilots to validate sight-lines and reach-zones immersively.
